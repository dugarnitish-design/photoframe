How to add photos

Easiest: a Google Photos shared album

In the Google Photos app, open (or create) an album → tap Share → Create link. Copy that link and paste it into the “Google Photos shared album” box above. The frame plays every photo in the album, and shows new ones you add later. The album must be shared as anyone with the link.
Note: this reads the public album page through a free helper service. It works in most cases; if it ever stops, use the direct links below as a backup.

Most reliable: imgbb.com (any single photo)

Go to imgbb.com, upload your photo, then copy the “Direct link” into an “Individual photo links” row. Works for photos from anywhere — Facebook, Instagram, or your phone.

Facebook & Instagram

These don't give stable direct links. Save the photo to your device, then upload to imgbb.com and copy the direct link.

Dropbox

Get a share link (ends in ?dl=0) and change the ending to ?raw=1. Example:
...photo.jpg?raw=1

Google Drive

Share the file as “Anyone with the link”, copy the file ID from the share URL, then use:
https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=FILE_ID

Tip: paste a link into your browser first. If you see only the photo, it'll work here.
